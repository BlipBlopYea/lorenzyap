About Me

Hi, I’m Lorenz. By day, I work as a Systems Administrator, where I’ve spent years mastering the intricate balance of IT infrastructure, stability, and problem-solving. My career in tech has taught me the importance of precision, technical discipline, and seeing the unseen details—skills that I’ve found are just as essential when I’m out in the field with my camera.

My transition into photography was a natural evolution. While my professional life is defined by systems and logic, photography has become my refuge. It’s the place where I trade server racks for the rhythm of nature, and where I apply my analytical eye to the nuances of light, composition, and the environment. Whether I'm optimizing a network or setting up the perfect shot, my goal remains the same: to find the beauty in complexity and document the world with intent.

Photography is more than just clicking a shutter for me; it’s about storytelling and patience. I thrive on the technical side of the craft—from understanding my gear to the intricate process of post-processing—and I love using those technical skills to capture moments in nature that would otherwise pass us by.

I’m constantly learning, and I invite you to join me as I continue to explore the intersection of technology and the natural world through my lens.
